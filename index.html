What Problem Needs to Be Solved?

Access to sustainable cooking solutions is limited in areas without conventional energy.
A solar oven provides clean, renewable cooking methods for communities in need.

Design Requirements

For a functional solar oven: efficient heat retention, insulation, light reflection, and portability are essential.
These ensure usability and performance under different conditions.
